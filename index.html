Michael Schumacher

The best Formula 1 driver of all time

This is what makes Michael Schumacher the best:

  • Made his debut at SPA, Belgium in 1991. Came in without much testing, and still qualified seventh.
  • Moved to Benetton for the very next race, and outperformed three-time champion Nelson Piquet.
  • Won his first race in Portugal, in 1992.
  • Stormed to victory in the first 4 races of 1994. Won the championship in 1994 and 1995, despite not having the fastest car.
  • In the 1994 Spanish Grand Prix, he finished a brilliant second place despite being stuck in fifth gear for most part of the race.
  • Moved to Ferrari in 1996, even though Ferrari were struggling at that time. Built the team around him and set about dominating Formula 1.
  • Won the 1996 Spanish Grand Prix, in incredibly wet conditions. Was 3 to 4 seconds faster that anyone else.
  • Took the title fight down to the wire in 1997 and 1998, despite having a inferior car.
  • Broke his leg, in a crash at the 1999 British Grand Prix. Came back to end the year as the fastest driver.
  • Won the championship, for Ferrari, in 2000. Became the first Ferrari driver to win in 21 years.
  • Dominated the sport for the next five years, winning the championship from 2000 to 2004.
